Cecilia Han Springer, Ph.D.

Research Fellow, Harvard Kennedy School Belfer Center

Harvard University

 

 

I am a research fellow at the Harvard Kennedy School's Belfer Center, jointly appointed at the Science, Technology, and Public Policy Program and the Environment and Natural Resources Program. Bridging energy, environment, and development, my research seeks to identify sustainable and just policies for energy systems in China and around the world. I am an interdisciplinary researcher, merging economics and human geography by combining economic modeling, econometric analysis, and qualitative field work.
